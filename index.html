About

With over 20 years of experience in hospitality operations, I've seen firsthand the challenges that venues face daily — from managing complex systems to maintaining operational efficiency while delivering exceptional customer experiences.

This deep industry knowledge sparked my transition into AI and software development, where I focus on creating practical solutions that bridge the gap between traditional operations and modern technology. Currently pursuing a BSc in Computing & AI at the Open University, I'm expanding my technical expertise while maintaining a strong foundation in real-world problem-solving.

My portfolio showcases a unique blend of practical AI tools designed to solve genuine industry problems and creative prototypes that explore the boundaries of what's possible. Every project is built with the understanding that technology should enhance human capabilities, not replace them.

Through PrettyNerdee, I'm committed to developing AI solutions that are accessible, practical, and genuinely useful for the people and businesses that need them most.
